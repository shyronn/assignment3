July 8, 1992

New York, NY.

6 NYU professors went in search for the best set of headphones for the department’s new recording studio, but when they couldn’t find what they were looking for, they decided to embark on a new side project: to create the best headphones the music market has ever seen. The side job quickly commanded all of their attention, and Box Headphones was born.

Steve Jacob, Product Developer.

Born in Detroit, Michigan. Steve develops new product technology and special edition headphones. Steve also sings in a band called Nitro with six other members that were featured in Rolling Stone magazine.

Sarah Wilson, Marketing.

Responsible for all promotional material and branding across the product range. Sarah is also a freelance writer for the music magazine, Red.

Steve Dixon, Engineer.

Knowing the latest in technology is very important to us. It keeps us ahead of the crowd, and allows Box Headphones to be the best in its category. Steve mixes music for animation films in his spare time. His latest film, Lion, has won several awards.

Ashley Thomas, Materials Management.

Ashley manages all range dispersement in major and boutique retail environments across North America and abroad. Ashley is the singer of the company, but she openly admits that she’s better behind a desk then a mic.

Blair Davis, Accountant.

Blair makes sure Box Headphones is operating effectively and efficiently. Blair is the drummer for the rock band Lift.

Tracy Mills, Office Manager.

Tracy keeps us all in line. She manages the entire office and keeps us informed of the office goals and time lines. Every month, Tracy hand picks a new artist to the office to perform in our lobby to give artist exposure and remind us all of the passion that is at the heart of Box Headphones.
